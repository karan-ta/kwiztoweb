Given:
ABCD is a parallelogram
To Prove:
ABCD is a rhombus
Proof:
AP = AS = x
SD = DR = y
PB = BQ = a
CR = CQ = b
External Tangents from a point to circle are equal.
AB = AP + PB = x + a
AD = AS + SD = x + y
BC = BQ + QC = a + b
CD = CR + RD = y + b
Points A,P,B are collinear.
Points A,S,D are collinear.
Points B,Q,C are collinear.
Points C,R,D are collinear.
AB + CD = x+a+y+b
AD + BC = x+a+y+b
∴ AB + CD = AD + BC,
From previous point, adding two sides.
AB = CD , AD = BC
2AB = 2AD and also:
2CD = 2BC
AB = AD and CD = BC

opposite sides of parallelogram are equal.
AB = AD = CD = BC
and hence
ABCD is a rhombus.
opposite and adjacent sides of quadrilateral ABCD are equal.
